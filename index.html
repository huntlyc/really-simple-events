Really Simple Events

Simple Events!

Simple event module, just a title and start date/time needed! You can, of course, provide extra information about the event if you wish. This plugin was created for bands/performers who do one-off shows lasting a couple of hours.

Download Now

Simple Event Table

Simple list of your events, you can add custom links and extra information if required. You can even choose to show only upcoming/past or all of your events!

Simple Creation

Quickly and easlily add your events! Most fields are optional. Easily add extra information by using the HTML editor, it's just the same as creating pages and posts!

Sidebar Widget

Everyone loves widgets! You can have one too! Customize the title, the number of events, you can even set a link to your events page, or not, its up to you!
